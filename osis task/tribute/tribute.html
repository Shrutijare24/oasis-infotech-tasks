Chatrapati Sambhaji Maharaj



Sambhaji was the son of Shivaji Maharaj,the first Chhatrapati of the Maratha Empire and his first wife Saibai. After Saibai’s death (1659), it is said that he was raised by Shivaji’s mother Jijabai. When he was nine years of age, he was sent to live as a political hostage at the palace of Raja Jai Singh I of Amber. Shivaji had to do this as surety that he would comply with the Treaty of Purandar he signed with the Mughals in 1665.
